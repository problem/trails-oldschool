How much would you pay?

Trails tracks time.

It aims to provide an honest window into your work.

Time is scarce, and time is money.

We spend our time on various tasks, yet so often there is no money or tangible result to show for it. The day slips away as the frantic mind is busy being busy.

There are those days, too, when we work on projects that offer monetary gain or other forms of satisfaction; we are intent and productive. We feel accomplished.

With these problems in mind, Trails was born:

In the most humble and simple of ways, Trails aims to help you identify which tasks are making you money, which tasks will make you money, help you keep track of what needs to be done, and generally assist in keeping you “on top of your shit.”

Trails will not solve all your problems. It will not put jam on your toast. In fact, it won’t do anything for you if you don’t use it.
